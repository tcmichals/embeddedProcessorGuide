Getting started

First time buyer?

  • Easiest and simplest is just get a board from ST Micro

    • The boards have a range of processors and peripherals and all have STLINK (SWD)

    • Documentation and schematic

  • Custom boards

    • Most do not have STLINK on board, must use an external cable

      • Look for Trace Pins, these are vary helpful (more on that later)

    • Some boards do not have good documentation or schematics
